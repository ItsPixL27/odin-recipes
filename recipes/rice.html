Vegetable Pulao

Vegetable Pulao is a fragrant and flavorful Indian rice dish made with basmati rice, mixed vegetables, and aromatic spices. It's a one-pot meal that's easy to prepare and perfect for serving as a main course or as a side dish with your favorite curry or yogurt raita.

  • Basmati Rice
  • Mixed Vegetables (such as carrots, peas, bell peppers, and potatoes), diced
  • Onion, thinly sliced
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Whole Spices (such as cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and bay leaves)
  • Ground Spices (such as turmeric, cumin, coriander, and garam masala)
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • Oil or ghee (clarified butter) for cooking
  • Salt to taste
  1. Wash the basmati rice under running water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a large pan or pressure cooker over medium heat.
  3. Add the whole spices (cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and bay leaves) and sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the thinly sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  5. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute.
  6. Add the mixed vegetables to the pan and sauté for a few minutes until they are slightly tender.
  7. Season with ground spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander, and garam masala) and salt. Mix well to coat the vegetables.
  8. Add the soaked and drained basmati rice to the pan. Stir gently to combine with the vegetables.
  9. Pour in water (use a 1:2 ratio of rice to water) and bring to a boil.
  10. Cover the pan with a tight-fitting lid and reduce the heat to low. Let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  11. Remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for another 5 minutes.
  12. Fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with chopped cilantro leaves before serving.
  13. Serve hot as a delicious and aromatic main course or side dish.
