Paneer Subji

Paneer Subji is a popular Indian dish featuring paneer, a type of Indian cottage cheese, cooked with a flavorful blend of spices and vegetables. This vegetarian delight is rich, creamy, and bursting with aromatic flavors. It's perfect for serving with rice or bread for a satisfying and wholesome meal.

    1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan over medium heat.
    2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
    3. Add minced ginger, garlic, and green chilies. Sauté for another minute.
    4. Stir in the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and release their juices.
    5. Add the spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala) and salt. Cook for a couple of minutes until fragrant.
    6. Gently add the cubed paneer to the pan and mix well with the masala (spice mixture).
    7. Cover and simmer for 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
    8. Remove the lid and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to thicken the gravy.
    9. Garnish with chopped cilantro leaves before serving.
    10. Serve hot with rice or bread of your choice.
  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cubed
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Tomatoes, chopped
  • Ginger, minced
  • Garlic, minced
  • Green chilies, chopped
  • Spices (such as turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala)
  • Fresh cilantro leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • Oil or ghee (clarified butter) for cooking
  • Salt to taste
  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add minced ginger, garlic, and green chilies. Sauté for another minute.
  4. Stir in the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and release their juices.
  5. Add the spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala) and salt. Cook for a couple of minutes until fragrant.
  6. Gently add the cubed paneer to the pan and mix well with the masala (spice mixture).
  7. Cover and simmer for 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  8. Remove the lid and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to thicken the gravy.
  9. Garnish with chopped cilantro leaves before serving.
  10. Serve hot with rice or bread of your choice.
